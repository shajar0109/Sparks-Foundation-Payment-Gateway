Sparks Funds

We have all been touched by disease. A father lost to cancer, or a mother to Alzheimer’s. A sister scarred by breast cancer, or a friend living with heart disease. In these moments, our thoughts of genomes and DNA are fleeting at most. We are consumed in giving comfort, trying to restore order, or grieving. At Coriell, we believe answers can be found in our genome. We are relentless in our investigation to crack those codes to understand why and how disease happens. We do it so our children and our grandchildren will not lose their father to cancer, their mother to Alzheimer’s, their friends to heart disease. Please join us. Let’s find answers together.
